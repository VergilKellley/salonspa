Our Story

angelhair

Citing the casual glamour that defines Chicago as a strong motivating factor behind his move here from London 30 years ago, Melvin—who started his career in an environment of incomparable exclusivity, the Queen’s hair salon—strove to channel Chicagos’s quintessential relaxed vibe in the launch of angelhair, which Melissa agrees is not your typical mid-western establishment. “The environment we’ve created at angelhair is a comfortable, carefree one,” she said. “We want the experience to be fun for everyone.” Speaking of the environment inside, not a detail was overlooked in making it one of refined visual beauty. Impeccably chosen finishes—rich walnut and luxe marble, for instance—establish an esthetic that juxtaposes the city’s requisite opulence with the comfort and whimsicality of which Melissa speaks.

ourteam

Melvin Sanchez

Principal & Stylist

A London native who began his career in a place of unrivaled prestige – the Queen’s salon – Melvin always admired the effortlessly stylish allure of New York. Upon relocating to the Chicago area decades ago, he quickly made a name for himself as a premier cutter and stylist – and along the way, at Cut ME salon, he established a lifelong friendship with Tracey Belgrade. For most of the last decade, Melvin was one-half of the force behind Melvin''s' salon. Today, he’s channeled his years of expertise and distinct sense of style to introduce angelhair, Chicago's premier full-service salon.

Melissa Delgado

Stylist

Is it any wonder that Melissa Delgado—a noted bangs-cutting expert who employs a strict level of precision in every style she creates—draws a great deal of her inspiration from 1960s style icons like Jane Birkin along with musical acts like Patti Smith and the Talking Heads? Her style is eclectic and fresh, and because she’s got a knack for tailoring it to suit the unique esthetic of each and every one of her clients, it’s placed her among the upper tier of Chicago's elite haircutting professionals. Having gotten her start at Sally Hershberger at John Frieda, Melissa worked at Neil George salon for seven years before angelhair opened its doors in 2012. Over the course of her career, she’s become known for her meticulous attention to detail, which includes not only dry-cutting the hair to ensure the utmost in texture and movement, but sharing with her clients helpful tips and tricks on making the most of their cuts once they leave the salon.

David Smith

Stylist

An expert in the art of precision cutting, David trained at Santa Monica’s Vidal Sassoon Academy before launching his career at the Vidal Sassoon Salon, where he spent more than a decade making a name for himself as a premier hair stylist. Citing the individual as his foremost muse, with music, art and fashion also playing an influential role in his creativity, he firmly believes that beauty differs from person to person, and strives to create easily maintained styles that bring out his clients’ unique natural beauty. Although he’s adept when it comes to women’s hair, he also enjoys cutting men’s hair, and over the years has amassed a lengthy list of loyal clients who demand his superior barber services.
